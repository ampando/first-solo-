About Me

Thanks for stopping by my page. I am new to the programming world, so please excuse the rough edges. I am coming from a career in the wine industry and after twenty plus years in hospitality I have decided to make a shift into something completely new.

Why Tech, you ask?

One of the many repercussions of the Pandemic was the crumbling of the restaurant industry. Typically, in good times and bad, people always eat and drink, but for the first time, in a long while, restaurants were required to shutter. I was personally out of work for a year and a half. During that time I was able deeply reflect, on everything. I turned forty, around the same time and my priorities shifted in life-changing ways. I decided to give up alcohol, to be the healthiest version of me. I also went back to college to finish my bachelor's degree in Psychology, something which I never felt was necessary to do while studying and working in food and wine. The time came for me to pick a new direction for my future. Restaurants have never been known for their stability and security, but they are now a main driving force for me, and pushed me to choose Epicodus on the recommendation of a former restaurant industry friend and recent graduate, now working in tech.
